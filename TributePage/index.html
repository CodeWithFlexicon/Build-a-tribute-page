Accomplishments of The World's Strongest Man

  • 1988 - Born in Reykjavík, Iceland
  • 2004 - Hafþór began his athletic career as a basketball player, playing as a center with a bodyweight of around 105 kg (231 lb). He started his senior team career for the Icelandic 1. deild karla club Breiðablik.
  • May 2004 - Won the Nordic Championship with the U-16 team
  • 2006 - Won the Nordic Championship with the U-18 team
  • 2008 - Began his Strongman career at 20 years-old at 6 ft 9 in, 140 kg (308 lb)
  • 2009 - Placed in the top 3 of multiple Strongman competitions
  • 2011 - Won his first Iceland's Strongest Man title
  • 2013 to 2015 - He competed prolifically winning eight international titles, three silver medals, and two bronze medals
